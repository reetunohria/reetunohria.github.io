About Me

Hi there! My name is Reetu, and I am a software engineer. My journey into the world of software development began during my high school years. As a junior, I enrolled in a Visual Basic course that ignited my passion for programming. I was hooked and decided to take a C++ course as a senior. What drew me in were the endless opportunities for learning, problem-solving and continuous sense of growth.

During those formative years, I discovered the extensive practicality of programming. I even started creating programs on my TI-85 calculator, using them to perform tasks like calculate exam grades needed to achieve specific semester targets. To my delight, other students became interested in these programs, and it was truly rewarding to see them being used by others.

Motivated by my early experiences, I made the decision to pursue Computer Science for both my bachelor's and master's degrees. Since then, my professional journey has taken me across diverse industries, ranging from defense to gaming to fintech. Along this path, I have worked with multiple technologies including Java, Python, JavaScript, React, Vue and MySQL for creating web applications, as well as Qt and C++ for building intuitive graphical user interfaces.

With every project, I strive to embrace the excitement of learning, solving complex challenges and witnessing the positive impact that software solutions can have on people's lives. My journey in programming has been an incredible adventure, and I look forward to continuing my exploration and creating meaningful applications.
